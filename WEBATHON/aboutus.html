Who Are We?

We are a bunch of nerds trying to help out those who have lost jobs due to the pandemic by donating our pocket money, redirecting donations like books, food, clothing, anything of value to them.

What do we Strive to Acheive?

Be more human and help those in need as well as make those who are living better off in this pandemic be more human and support our cause.

How you can help those in need?

Donate the things in your house that may not be of value to you but can be of much help to the poor and jobless; Clothes, books, any resource that can be helpful. If you donate financially, a Big Thumbs Up from us!

Why you can trust us?

Unlike any other organization, we are transparent in our work. You may contact us if you are not sure how you can trust us and we will show you how you can!
